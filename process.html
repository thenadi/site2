Process

The Nadhi creates designs by hand picking high-quality raw materials, inspires regular use & prevent waste.

Our fabrics are made by hand to not just ensure high quality but also preserve environment by using natural energy, & less water.

To reduce the carbon emission, water consumption, & pollution we use 100% rain-fed organic cotton, recycled fibers, & natural dyes.
